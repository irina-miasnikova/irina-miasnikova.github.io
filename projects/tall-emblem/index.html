Tall Emblem Structure in Za’abeel Park in Dubai

Located on the territory of Zaabbel Park, the structure should contribute to the development of tourism, have a unique artistic design, include structures for cultural and recreational activities, as well as have rooms for conferences and theatrical events. Local climatic conditions as well as urban planning and landscape aspects should be taken into account; when creating a project, passive structures should not be used, and the structure itself must meet the seismic conditions of the region.
